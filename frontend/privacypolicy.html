Privacy Policy

Learn how EuroQuest collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Introduction

At EuroQuest Recruitment Agency, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all individuals who interact with our services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you apply for jobs, contact us, or browse our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including, but not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • CV/Resume and documents
  • Passport and identification documents
  • Job preferences and work history

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used for the following purposes:

  • Processing job applications
  • Communicating with you
  • Verifying documents for overseas placement
  • Coordinating with partner employers
  • Internal record keeping

3. Data Protection

We implement strict administrative, technical, and physical security measures to safeguard personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, loss, or misuse.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Partner employers in Europe (for recruitment purposes)
  • Government authorities when required by law
  • Trusted third‑party service providers assisting our operations

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. You may contact us anytime to exercise these rights.

6. Changes to This Policy

EuroQuest may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, contact us at:
