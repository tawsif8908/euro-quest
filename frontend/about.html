About EuroQuest

Your trusted bridge between Bangladesh and Europe — building careers, empowering families, and supporting European employers with skilled manpower.

Who We Are

EuroQuest is a professional manpower recruitment agency connecting skilled workers from Bangladesh to European countries such as Romania, Moldova, Cyprus, and beyond. With years of experience in the recruitment industry, we ensure that both job seekers and employers experience a smooth and transparent hiring process.

We focus on ethical recruitment, proper document processing, visa support, travel assistance, and complete end‑to‑end manpower solutions.

Message from the CEO

“At EuroQuest, our mission is simple — connect talented individuals with the right opportunities. We understand the dreams of Bangladeshi workers and the needs of European employers. With a committed team operating from Romania, we ensure that every step of the recruitment process is handled with honesty, transparency, and professionalism.”

— CEO, EuroQuest
