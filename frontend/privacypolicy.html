Privacy Policy

How EuroQuest collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Introduction

EuroQuest Recruitment Agency values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • CV/Resume documents
  • Passport and ID details
  • Job preferences & work history

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing your job applications
  • Verifying documents for overseas placement
  • Coordinating with employers and partners
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Improving our agency services

3. Data Protection Measures

We use secure servers, encryption, and strict administrative controls to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse of personal data.

4. Sharing of Information

Your personal data is NEVER sold. It may only be shared with:

  • European employers (strictly for job processing)
  • Government authorities (when required by law)
  • Trusted third‑party service providers

5. Your Rights

You may request access, corrections, or deletion of your personal information anytime.

6. Policy Updates

EuroQuest may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here.
