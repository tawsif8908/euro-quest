Privacy Policy

How EuroQuest collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Introduction

EuroQuest Recruitment Agency is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Uploaded documents (CV, passport, certificates)
  • Job preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your job application
  • Communicate with you
  • Verify identity and documents
  • Forward your profile to European employers

3. Data Protection

We use encrypted systems and strict access controls to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Information Sharing

We share your information ONLY with:

  • Verified employers
  • Government authorities (legal purposes)
  • Trusted third‑party service providers

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information anytime.

6. Updates

Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted on this page.
